UI/UX DESIGN

Opportunity

Mogul Mentorship & Courses provides lessons and one-on-one mentorship to students who subscribe to the service. I was tasked to both redesign this portion of Mogul’s website and create an e-mail drip campaign in order to increase revenue and conversions.





Competitor Analysis

I conducted an in-depth analysis on how other subscription-based startups engaged users and how their products features were marketed.

Addtionally, I reviewed strategies utilized by companies who created successful email campaigns with high conversion rates in order to see if the same stragies could be implemented in this email drip campaign.



Final Prototype & Testing

Design assets were created in Sketch. Email coding and further iteraction were finalized in Sendgrid for the email campaign. Emails were tested across multiple email clients including Outlook, Gmail, and Apple Mail.
